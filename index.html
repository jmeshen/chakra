Visualizing the 7 Chakras

Chakras are the energy centers in our body through which energy flows.
When our chakras are out of balance, blocked energy can often lead to illness.
Choose a color below to learn about opening each chakra.

root chakra

The 1st Chakra, the Root Chakra, represents our foundation and is all about feeling grounded and secure.

Location: Base of spine in tailbone area.

Emotional Issues: Survival issues such as financial independence, money, and food.

Healing Exercises:

  • stomping the ground with your bare feet
  • yoga: bridge pose

Healing Foods:

  • red-colored food: apples, beets
  • hot spices: cayenne pepper, Tabasco sauce
  • ground vegetables: potatoes, carrots
  • animal proteins: red meat, eggs

sacral chakra

The 2nd Chakra, the Sacral Chakra, relates to our ability to feel creative, sexual, and accept change.

Location: Lower abdomen, about 2 inches below the navel and 2 inches in.

Emotional Issues: Sense of abundance, well-being, pleasure, sexuality.

Healing Exercises:

  • pelvic thrusts
  • yoga: cobra pose

Healing Foods:

  • orange-colored food: oranges, tangerines
  • nuts

solar plexus chakra

The 3rd Chakra, the Solar Plexus Chakra, represents our ability to be confident and in control of our lives.

Location: Upper abdomen in the stomach area.

Emotional Issues: Self-worth, self-confidence, self-esteem.

Healing Exercises:

  • dancing
  • yoga: boat pose

Healing Foods:

  • yellow-colored food: corn
  • grains and fiber: granola, whole wheat bread
  • peppermint and chamomile tea

heart chakra

The 4th Chakra, the Heart Chakra, represents our ability to love — the quality of our love, our past loves — and our future loves.

Location: Center of chest just above heart.

Emotional Issues: Love, joy, inner peace.

Healing Exercises:

  • bikram yoga
  • love: open up your heart to others

Healing Foods:

  • green-colored food: leafy vegetables, spinach
  • green tea

throat chakra

The 5th Chakra, the Throat Chakra, represents our ability to communicate and express ourselves.

Location: Throat.

Emotional Issues: Communication, self-expression of feelings, speaking the truth, holding secrets.

Healing Exercises:

  • shoulder stand
  • singing, chanting

Healing Foods:

  • juices and teas
  • all types of fruit

third-eye chakra

The 6th Chakra, the Third-Eye Chakra, is related to our ability to focus on and see the big picture.

Location: Forehead between the eyes.

Emotional Issues: Intuition, imagination, wisdom, ability to think and make decisions.

Healing Exercises:

  • yoga: child's pose, poses with forward bends
  • eye exercises
  • herbal oil treatment

Healing Foods:

  • purple-colored fruits: grapes, blueberries
  • chocolate
  • lavender flavored spices or tea

crown chakra

The 7th and highest Chakra, the Crown Chakra, represents our ability to be fully connected spiritually.

Location: The very top of the head.

Emotional Issues: Inner and outer beauty, our connection to spirituality, pure bliss.

Healing:

  • meditation
  • cardio: running
  • fresh air and sunshine
